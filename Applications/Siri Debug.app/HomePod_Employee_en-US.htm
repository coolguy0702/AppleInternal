WHY IS THIS STUDY BEING DONE? This study is being done to understand and improve Siri and HomePod in everyday use while in the home and related Apple products and services.

 

WHAT WILL HAPPEN IF I TAKE PART IN THIS STUDY?

á       You will receive instructions for use of the unit and security protocols for living on the device.

á       You will be allocated a LiveOn Kit if you do not already have one.

á       You will be asked to use these units as your primary HomePod device for approximately 1 year.

á       You will install a custom profile on your iOS device that configures Siri to send data to Siri servers for the study.  This will allow you to identify and distinguish individual speakers.

á       So we can better understand and improve your experience, we will collect data from your use of the device, and you may be asked to file/submit feedback (e.g., Siri Debug App and Tap-to-Radar).

á       You may be contacted directly for follow up on any reported issues.

á       You will be asked to use the device in your everyday living, triggering Siri an average of 4-5 times per day. Please note that we may decrease the confidence threshold for collecting ÒHey SiriÓ utterances as compared to production HomePod devices in order to determine the most accurate threshold.  This may lead to  the collection of additional utterances that are acoustically similar to ÒHey SiriÓ.  We may also collect utterances up to 2 seconds before ÒHey SiriÓ is recognized.  Other than as described in this consent, data collected through the HomePod will generally be collected in accordance with production HomePod devices.

á       You will be asked to listen to the HomePod utterances in Siri Debug App and label the data with a user name (you or your family members who have signed the consent form). You should only use your first name or the first name of your family memberÕs name when labeling the data. For non-family members, you must label the utterances as Òguest.Ó

á        You will be able to review all utterances prior to submitting. Before submitting, you should review to make sure that the data in the Siri Debug App does not include any sensitive or confidential information.

 

 

HOW LONG WILL MY PARTICIPATION IN THIS STUDY TAKE? You will live on the device for approximately one year. Data from the study will be retained longer as described below.

For more information on how Study Data will be kept secure and confidential, please contact the Study Coordinator listed above.
